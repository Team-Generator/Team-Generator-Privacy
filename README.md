# Team-Generator Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for the Team-Generator app.

## Privacy Policy

Team-Generator helps users organize classes by creating groups, teams, class maps, seating arrangements, saved group sets, and user-created notes or planning documents. Users can enter names manually or extract names from class lists using the device camera or images selected by the user.

## Data Collection

Team-Generator does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or third parties.

All class information, names, groups, rules, class maps, seating arrangements, notes, planning documents, and other user-created content are stored locally on the user’s device. The developer does not have access to this data.

## Names, Groups, Rules, and Class Maps

The app processes information entered by the user, such as names, group rules, class maps, seating arrangements, saved groups, and related class information, in order to generate groups, teams, class maps, and seating placements.

This information stays on the user’s device and is not sent to the developer or third parties.

## Notes and Planning Documents

If the app includes notes, planning templates, or planning documents, these are created and stored locally by the user. The developer does not access, collect, or transmit the content of these notes or documents.

Users are responsible for the information they choose to enter into the app.

## Camera and Selected Images

If the user chooses to scan a class list, the app requests camera access. The camera is used to scan class lists and extract names.

The app can also analyze images selected by the user from the device’s photo library. Only images chosen by the user are accessed. Images are analyzed on device to extract names and are not saved or sent to the developer or third parties.

## Import and Export

Team-Generator may allow users to import and export class data, groups, rules, class maps, notes, or other user-created content.

Exported files may contain information entered by the user. Users are responsible for how they store, share, or transfer exported files.

## Third Parties

Team-Generator does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external data collection services.
