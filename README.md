# Team-Generator Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for the Team-Generator app.

## Privacy Policy

Team-Generator helps users create groups and teams from names entered manually or extracted from class lists using the device camera or images selected by the user.

## Data Collection

Team-Generator does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties.

## Names and Groups

The app processes names entered by the user or extracted from selected images in order to create groups and teams. This information stays on the user’s device and is not sent to the developer or third parties.

## Camera and Selected Images

If you choose to scan a class list, the app requests camera access. The camera is used to scan class lists and extract names.

The app can also analyze images selected by the user from the device’s photo library. Only images chosen by the user are accessed. Images are analyzed on device to extract names and are not saved or sent to the developer or third parties.

## Third Parties

Team-Generator does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external data collection services.
